Batch 1

MYSELF is a calming and personal character play experience. Inspired by traditional paper dolls, the adult PETIT characters come in simple, modular, and neutral shapes.

This first batch features two sets of characters with basic settings and items: hair, clothing, accessories, flowers, and coins. All elements are designed to be assembled, removed, and revised (just like identities are constantly being reshaped).
